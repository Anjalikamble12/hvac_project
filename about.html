Our Journey

Aria Climate Technique was founded in Pune, India, with a vision to transform the HVAC&R industry through innovative solutions and unparalleled service. Established by a team of seasoned professionals—Subodh Murkewar, Virendra Borade, and Chetan Thakur—our company emerged from a shared passion for enhancing indoor environments and promoting energy efficiency.

Founding Vision

From the outset, our mission has been clear: to provide high-quality, cost-effective HVAC&R solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. With decades of combined experience in the field, our founders recognized the growing demand for reliable and efficient climate control systems across various sectors, including industrial, commercial, and healthcare.

Milestones and Growth

Project Diversification: We have successfully executed numerous projects, ranging from industrial facilities to sophisticated clean rooms for healthcare applications.
Technological Advancements: Embracing the latest technologies, we have continually updated our service offerings to incorporate energy-efficient solutions and smart system designs.
Client Relationships: Building lasting partnerships with our clients has been a cornerstone of our growth. We pride ourselves on understanding their unique requirements and delivering tailored solutions that exceed expectations.

Commitment to Quality

At Aria Climate Technique, quality is not just a promise; it's a practice. We adhere to the highest industry standards, ensuring that every project is executed with meticulous attention to detail. Our commitment to quality has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner in the HVAC&R industry.

Community Engagement

As we grow, we remain dedicated to our community. We believe in giving back and actively participate in initiatives that promote sustainability and energy conservation, aligning our business practices with broader environmental goals.
